About

Sherwood Interact is a group of students, ages 12-18 interested in making a positive impact in the community, both locally and globally, who participate in service projects that work toward social justice. Sherwood Interact is sponsored by the Rotary Club of Sherwood. Rotary is a global network of community volunteers that helps build goodwill and peace through programs that address illiteracy, disease, hunger, poverty, lack of clean water, and environmental concerns (among others). The Rotary Club of Sherwood has an annual tree sale fundraiser that supports programs in our community. Last year the Rotary Club was able to sponsor new Peace Poles at the YMCA and schools (to come summer 2016), Interact, Friends of the Senior Center, Loaves and Fishes, Sherwood High Construction Program, Sherwood FFA, YMCA Teen Center, Faith in Action, Boy scout programs, scholarships for high school students and the Rotary Youth Leadership Awards. To serve internationally, the club supports an ongoing service project in La Plata, Columbia and Youth Exchange students. Each year, Sherwood Interact hosts a school wide talent show to support an organization of their choice. With this talent show, Interact is able to donate around $2,500. When you join Interact, you become a part of a network of volunteers that serves to make the world a better place. Your ONE voice becomes a chorus of others around you to make a tremendous impact in the world.
